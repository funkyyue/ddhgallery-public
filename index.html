Submit Your Research

Share Your Work

We welcome submissions of chip research and publications from researchers worldwide. To submit your work for inclusion in the DDH Gallery, please contact us directly.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Original research in chip design, architecture, or related fields
  • Published or accepted papers from conferences, journals, or ArXiv
  • High-quality chip images or diagrams
  • Complete author information and publication details
  • Permission to display the work publicly

How to Submit:

  1. Email us at contact@ddhgallery.com
  2. Include your paper details and chip images
  3. We'll review and add your work to the gallery
  4. You'll be notified once your submission is live
